Grace Davidson-Lynch

Grace is a writer, dramaturg, and educator based in Sydney. She studied at the University of Wollongong, where she wrote for IPAC’s Drama Studio. She was also a member of Merrigong’s Playwright Project in 2019. In 2022, her play Hydrarchos was staged at NIDA’s Festival of Emerging Artists and longlisted for the Best in Theatre Award at Sydney Fringe Festival. In 2024, she took part in ATYP’s National Studio writer’s retreat. Grace has also worked as a dramaturg and story supervisor for the Dire Theatre Company, Matriark Theatre, Othelia Story Technologies, and Merrigong X. She has contributed her writing to BOOKER Magazine, Creative+Business, and her personal website, and has taught theatre studies at the University of Wollongong.
