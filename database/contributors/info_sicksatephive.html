Sicks Ate Phive is a theatre maker based on Dharug land in Sydney. They are a Pasifika theatre maker who is passionate about uplifting Pasifika voices, especially our stories. They have been involved in a range of University and independent theatre and work professionally as a researcher and Pacific Islander dancer.
Due to their relationship with their community and future projects they have chosen to remain anonymous for this project. Sicks Ate Phive hopes to show readers that whilst our industry is improving and growing, there is always uncertainty behind why.
