Grace Ellen Macpherson | Co-Artistic Director and Co-Editor of ORANGUTAN

Grace is a journalist and arts worker based in Sydney. She concurrently studied her Master of Theatre (Dramaturgy) and Master of Arts and Cultural Management at the University of Melbourne, achieving first class honours in both.

Grace has worked as an arts manager with major Australian arts festivals, including Sydney Festival and RISING Festival, as well as Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2024. She completed the News Corp National Editorial Cadet program between 2023-2024. Her freelance arts writing has been published in ArtsHub Australia, The Big Idea NZ, The Weekend Australian Review and Art Edit Magazine.
