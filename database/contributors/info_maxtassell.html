Max Tassell

Max Tassell is a director, writer, dramaturg, film watcher and book reader. In the course of studying and practicing theatre he has travelled to Indonesia and Vietnam, completed a secondment with the Sydney Theatre Company, and graduated from VCA with a Master of Theatre (Directing). Recently, he used a Protostars grant from the Blackbird foundation to travel and write in regional NSW, and is currently directing an upcoming short film written by Will Hall, to be shot in May. You can find him at maxtassell.substack.com and @mxt on Letterboxd.
